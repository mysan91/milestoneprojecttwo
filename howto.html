How to play Memory

1. Start by choosing a theme from the Main menu

2. When you have chosen the theme, you will come to a game surface containing 30 images.
Your mission is to find 2 matching pairs.

3. Start the game by flipping a card.

4. Then try to find another card that has the same image as the first

5. If you can't find a matching pair, the flipped cards will be flipped back with the face down.

6. Try to remember what the cards you just flipped contains.

7. Try again and find the matching pair, and now you know whats under the previously flipped cards so keep looking for the matching card.

8. When you find a pair the cards wont flip back again and the front of the card will continue to appear.

9. Now you keep on looking for all the pair in the game.

10. When you have find all the matching pair the game is complete and you can try again to beat your previous times and moves.

11. Now lets start with coing back to the main menu and chose theme of the game.

Good luck and have fun!
